What We Do?

We are a grassroots NGO dedicated to supporting people in need—especially children, families, and the elderly who lack access to food, education, and care. Our mission is to help where help is needed most, with practical support and compassion.

Started as a local effort by our founder Aditya Gupta, our work has grown into a community initiative reaching rural and low-income areas. We distribute food kits, help children go to school, and offer care to the neglected—always focusing on real needs on the ground.

We believe small actions can create big changes. Every effort is driven by honesty, empathy, and a commitment to making a difference—one life at a time.

Aditya's Journey: The Story Behind Swapan Kiran Trust

The Genesis of a Dream

Aditya grew up witnessing the struggles of his community firsthand. The lack of access to basic education and resources deeply impacted him, igniting a passion to make a tangible difference. This early exposure shaped his resolve to uplift those around him, laying the foundation for what would become Swapan Kiran Trust.

Building Bridges of Hope

Starting with humble beginnings, Aditya began by personally distributing food and teaching children under a tree. His dedication attracted like-minded individuals, and soon, his small effort blossomed into a community-wide initiative. This phase was marked by tireless work, building trust, and creating foundational programs.

A Vision Realized, A Future Brightened

Today, Swapan Kiran Trust stands as a beacon of hope, a direct result of Aditya's unwavering commitment. From providing education to ensuring food security, the trust continues to expand its reach, transforming countless lives and fostering a brighter future for the underprivileged. This is his legacy, built on compassion and action.
